Our Specialties

At Arabic Mandi, our specialty is the mandi, a traditional Middle Eastern dish that is known for its rich flavors and aromatic spices. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to prepare this dish, ensuring that each bite is bursting with flavor. We also offer a range of other specialties, including biriyani, starters, pizzas, and mocktails.

Biriyani

Aromatic basmati rice cooked with succulent chicken, caramelized onions, and an array of warming spices, garnished with fried onions and served with raita.

mandi

Tender lamb cooked to perfection with a blend of traditional Arabic spices and served atop fragrant basmati rice, garnished with almonds and grated carrots
